Chapter 0: The Beginning
Tema: Pengenalan Pembelajaran Bahasa Inggris

A. Kompetensi
Mengenal pentingnya belajar Bahasa Inggris

Memahami cara belajar Bahasa Inggris yang efektif

Menumbuhkan rasa percaya diri dalam menggunakan Bahasa Inggris di kelas

B. Grammar
Pengenalan pronouns (I, you, we, they)

Penggunaan "to be": is, am, are

C. Language Features
Kalimat kelas dasar: “May I go to the toilet?”, “I don’t understand”, “How do you say ... in English?”

Ekspresi partisipasi di kelas: listen, repeat, raise your hand

D. Vocabulary
Kosakata kelas: book, pen, teacher, whiteboard

Kata kerja dasar: read, write, speak, listen

Chapter 1: Greetings and Partings
Tema: Ungkapan salam dan perpisahan dalam berbagai situasi

A. Grammar
Kalimat sederhana dengan “to be”: “I am …”, “You are …”

Kalimat tanya: “How are you?”, “What is your name?”

B. Language Features
Greeting expressions: “Hello”, “Hi”, “Good morning”, “Nice to meet you”

Parting expressions: “Goodbye”, “See you later”, “Have a nice day”

C. Vocabulary
Waktu dalam salam: morning, afternoon, evening, night

Kata-kata sapaan: friend, teacher, classmate

Chapter 2: Thanking and Apologizing
Tema: Ungkapan terima kasih dan permintaan maaf

A. Grammar
Kalimat dengan subject + verb: “I thank you”, “I’m sorry”

Penggunaan modal expression: “May I…”, “Can I…”

B. Language Features
Thanking expressions: “Thank you very much”, “Thanks”, “I appreciate it”

Apologizing expressions: “I’m sorry”, “Excuse me”, “I didn’t mean to”

C. Vocabulary
Kata kerja sopan: apologize, forgive, help

Situasi sosial: late, borrow, disturb

Chapter 3: Introducing Oneself and Others
Tema: Memperkenalkan diri dan orang lain

A. Grammar
Kalimat perkenalan: “My name is…”, “I live in…”

Pronouns: I, you, he, she, they

To be: am, is, are

B. Language Features
Asking names: “What’s your name?”

Introducing others: “This is my friend…”, “May I introduce…?”

C. Vocabulary
Nama tempat: school, city, country

Kata sifat dasar: friendly, smart, nice

Chapter 4: My Family and Me
Tema: Mendeskripsikan diri dan anggota keluarga

A. Grammar
Possessive adjectives: my, your, his, her

Simple present tense (positive & negative): “She has a brother.” / “I don’t have a sister.”

B. Language Features
Describing people: “He is tall.”, “She has long hair.”

Asking about family: “How many brothers do you have?”

C. Vocabulary
Anggota keluarga: father, mother, cousin, uncle, aunt

Ciri fisik: tall, short, slim, chubby, black hair

Chapter 5: Numbers, Dates, and Time
Tema: Mengenal angka, tanggal, dan waktu

A. Grammar
Question forms: “What time is it?”, “When is your birthday?”

Prepositions of time: in, on, at

B. Language Features
Telling time: “It’s ten o’clock”, “It’s half past seven”

Talking about dates: “My birthday is on March 3rd.”

C. Vocabulary
Angka: one to one hundred

Hari dan bulan: Monday to Sunday, January to December

Waktu: hour, minute, morning, night

BAB 4: My School Activities
Tema: Kegiatan-Kegiatan Harian di Sekolah

A. Kompetensi
Menyebutkan dan menjelaskan kegiatan harian di sekolah

Menyusun kalimat sederhana tentang rutinitas dan waktu

B. Grammar (Tata Bahasa)
Simple Present Tense: untuk menyatakan kebiasaan ("I study at 8 a.m.")

Adverbs of Frequency: always, usually, sometimes, never

Prepositions of Time: in, on, at (contoh: "at 7 o'clock", "on Monday")

C. Language Features (Kebahasaan)
Pola kalimat positif dan negatif kegiatan sehari-hari

Penggunaan waktu dalam konteks kegiatan (before class, after break)

D. Vocabulary (Kosakata)
Kegiatan sekolah: study, read, write, discuss, sing, play, listen

Jadwal & waktu: Monday, Tuesday, 8 a.m., 12 o’clock, lunch break, flag ceremony

Tempat kegiatan: classroom, library, canteen, lab

BAB 5: This is My School
Tema: Mengenal dan Mendeskripsikan Lingkungan Sekolah

A. Kompetensi
Menunjukkan lokasi dan bagian-bagian sekolah

Mendeskripsikan tempat dan benda dengan kata sifat

B. Grammar
Prepositions of Place: in, on, under, next to, behind, in front of

Articles: a, an, the ("This is a classroom." / "The library is big.")

Demonstratives: this, that, these, those

C. Language Features
Kalimat deskriptif tempat dan lokasi ("The canteen is next to the office.")

Kalimat tanya tentang lokasi dan deskripsi ("Where is the library?", "What is it like?")

D. Vocabulary
Tempat di sekolah: classroom, school yard, teachers’ room, library, laboratory

Deskripsi tempat: clean, big, small, crowded, quiet

Alat bantu sekolah: board, eraser, marker, globe, microscope

BAB 6: Describing People, Objects, and Animals
Tema: Mendeskripsikan Orang, Benda, dan Hewan

A. Kompetensi
Mendeskripsikan penampilan fisik orang

Mendeskripsikan ciri-ciri benda dan hewan secara sederhana

B. Grammar
Adjectives: big, small, tall, short, kind, beautiful

To be + Adjective: "She is tall." / "It is fluffy."

Singular and Plural Nouns: "a dog", "three dogs"

C. Language Features
Deskripsi sederhana menggunakan adjective + noun

Kalimat tanya deskriptif: "What does he look like?" / "What is it?" / "How many legs does it have?"

D. Vocabulary
Deskripsi orang: short hair, long hair, dark skin, curly hair

Benda: book, pencil, chair, bottle, bag

Hewan: cat, dog, rabbit, elephant, bird

Ciri khas hewan: furry, big ears, long trunk, sharp teeth
